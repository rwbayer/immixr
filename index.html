About

immixr boosts visitor engagement by making it easy to incorporate an Instagram collage in your site.

The collage is customizable, allowing users to decide the distance between images, the size of the images, and the opacity of the images.

immixr expands to fill the height and width of its parent element, making it easy to create interesting backgrounds or a creative segue between sections on your site.

immixr makes it easy to integrate your Instagram feed into your page using a beautiful, customizable collage
